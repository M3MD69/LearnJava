# Some Logical Thinking

## Question and Answer

- **Original Numbers**

1, 2, 4, 9, 5, 3, 8, 7, 10, 12, 14

- **After Rearranged**

1, 4, 2, 9, 3, 8, 5, 10, 7, 14, 12

---

## **Why?**

- **False Thinking ❎**

```text
1, (2), 4, (9), 5, (3), 8, (7), 10, (12), 14

1, (2), (3), 4, 5, (7), 8, (9), 10, (12), 14

**Target Numbers**

2 3 7 9 12

**Not Target Numbers**

1 4 5 8 10 14
```

- **True Thinking ✅**

```text
1, (4), 2, (9), 3, (8), 5, (10), 7, (14), 12

1, (3), 2, (5), 4, (8), 7, (10), 9, (14), 12

**Target Numbers**

3 5 8 10 14 14

**Not Target Numbers**

1 2 4 7 9 12
```
